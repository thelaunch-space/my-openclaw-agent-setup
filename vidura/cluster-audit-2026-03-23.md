## Competitive Content Pattern Analysis

**What's working for competitors (validated across 22 days of tracking):**

### 1. Structured Comparison Tables
- Multiple dimensions (speed, cost, risk, scalability, best for)
- Real numbers (not vague ranges): "$50K-$150K fixed scope", "3-10 days ready teams", "90% faster, 80% cheaper"
- Clear best-for scenarios with specific conditions

### 2. Authority Signals
- Data citations: "YC research - companies lacking technical co-founder underperform"
- Real-world examples: "Pricing tables in 45 minutes", "Marketplace in 20 minutes"
- Proof points: "Saved bookkeepers 5+ hours/week", "Deployed MVP in under 10 minutes"

### 3. Actionable Frameworks
- Assessment questions: "Can you set technical direction? Are skills sufficient?"
- Decision matrices: "If X condition → choose Y option"
- Vetting checklists: "Referrals, case studies, references, premium pricing signals"

### 4. Complete Solution Stacks
- Not single tools, but full recommended stacks with pricing
- Example: "Carrd ($19/year) + Bubble (free-$349/month) + Zapier ($0-49/month) = $0-500/month total vs $20K-100K custom dev"
- Integration guidance: "Zapier connects 5,000+ apps"

### 5. Transition/Hybrid Strategies
- "Use agencies for MVP launch → hire in-house around validated product"
- "Build no-code MVP first → recruit cofounder if needed"
- "Start with free tiers (Bolt.new, Bubble) → upgrade when validated"

**What thelaunch.space content currently lacks:**
- Structured comparison tables with specific numbers
- Authority data citations (YC, industry research, founder surveys)
- Complete solution stacks with pricing breakdowns
- Assessment question frameworks
- Transition/hybrid strategy guidance
