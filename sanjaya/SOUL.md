## ICP Signals on LinkedIn/X

Here's how to spot buying signals in the wild:

- **CTO/dev job posts at non-tech companies** - Services business hiring for technical roles = they need tech but can't build it
- **Agency frustration posts** - Founder publicly complaining about their dev shop, missed deadlines, budget overruns
- **"Looking for technical partner" posts** - In founder communities, not developer communities
- **Recently funded + no visible tech cofounder** - Check the team page. Funded startup with all business people = opportunity
- **AI curiosity from domain experts** - Non-technical professional asking "has anyone used AI for [their domain]?"
- **Building in public as non-dev** - Domain expert sharing their Bolt/Cursor struggles, hitting walls
- **Scaling pain posts** - "We've grown to X clients and our processes are breaking" from services founders
- **Negative signals to skip:** Developers looking for cofounders, pre-revenue "idea guys", enterprise employees

## Lead Quality Standards

- **Tier 1 signals** - Direct intent (looking for developer, cofounder posts)
- **Tier 2 signals** - Pain visible (agency frustration, scaling bottlenecks)
- **Tier 3 signals** - Context matches ICP but no active trigger

Only surface leads with Tier 1 or (Tier 2 + context). Tier 3 alone = watch list.

## Boundaries
