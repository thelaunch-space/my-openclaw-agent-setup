### H5: Which Tier Makes Sense for Your Business

**Tier 1: Free Tools (Google Calendar, Trello)**
- **When:** Solo or 2-3 techs, under 15 jobs/week, simple scheduling
- **Cost:** $0
- **Trade-off:** Manual coordination, no automation, no mobile app for techs

**Tier 2: Mid-Tier SaaS (Housecall Pro, Jobber, FieldPulse, Connecteam)**
- **When:** 5-15 techs, 20-100 jobs/week, need mobile app + GPS + invoicing
- **Cost:** $79-$195/month ($948-$2,340/year)
- **Best for:** Most small service businesses (plumbing, HVAC, lawn care, cleaning)
- **ROI timeline:** 1-3 months (if manual is costing $30K+/year)

**Tier 3: Enterprise SaaS (ServiceTitan)**
- **When:** 15+ techs, complex multi-location operations, need advanced integrations
- **Cost:** $8K-$15K/year
- **Trade-off:** Overkill for small crews (you're paying for features you won't use)

**Tier 4: Custom Build**
- **When:** 
  - Off-the-shelf doesn't fit your specific workflow (custom pricing, unique integrations)
  - Per-user pricing kills margins (10+ techs = $2,000+/month SaaS vs. $15K one-time build)
  - You want to own the code and avoid SaaS lock-in
- **Cost:** $15K-$25K build + $200/month hosting
- **ROI timeline:** 12-24 months (pays for itself vs. SaaS over 2 years)
- **When it makes sense:** You've already validated your workflow with SaaS and know exactly what you need

### H7: Migration Strategy (How to Switch Without Chaos)

**Common fear:** "I can't afford downtime mid-season with 40 active jobs."

**Safe transition playbook:**
1. **Pilot with 1-2 techs first** (2 weeks) - validate software works before full rollout
2. **Run parallel for 2 weeks** - keep old system running while new system spins up (catch errors before you commit)
3. **Full cutover on a slow week** - don't migrate during peak season (choose shoulder season or slow month)
4. **Train techs on mobile app** - 30-min hands-on session per tech (not a 2-hour classroom lecture)
5. **Customer communication** - proactive email/text: "We're upgrading our scheduling system for better service" (turn migration into marketing moment)

**Timeline:** 4-6 weeks from purchase to full adoption (2 weeks pilot + 2 weeks parallel + 2 weeks stabilization)

**Cost of bad migration:** Rushing leads to missed jobs, frustrated techs, angry customers. Take the 6 weeks.
